# River Teign — priority spilling assets

*Partner briefing. Derived from River Hub: EA storm-overflow (EDM) annual returns 2020–2025, live
spill events 2026, and the PR24/PR19 WINEP dataset. Open EA data.*

## Headline
The Teign's sewage-overflow burden concentrates in **two clusters — Chagford and the Newton Abbot
area — plus a string of chronic rural treatment works** (Moretonhampstead, Dunsford, Heathfield,
Chudleigh, Christow). The biggest works have discharged for **thousands of hours a year**, and many
are **still spilling in 2026**.

## How to read this
- **Lifetime spills / hours** — summed EA annual returns 2020–2025 (regulator-verified).
- **2026** — spills recorded so far this year by the live EDM feed (evidence of continuing spills,
  not a full-year total).
- SO and SSO are two outfalls of one works — combined here.

## 3. The two problem clusters
- **Chagford** — Meldon Rd CSO, Chagford Fire 1 CSO, Greenacres CSO, Drewsteignton STW. A small town
  generating repeated 2026 CSO activity.
- **Newton Abbot / Teign estuary** — Keyberry Rd, Buckland Rd, Forde Park, Lemon Rd CSOs +
  Abbotskerswell, plus **Heathfield, Chudleigh, East Ogwell, Chudleigh Knighton STWs**. The largest
  cluster by asset count, discharging toward the **Teign Estuary shellfish water**.
- **Teign Valley** (Dunsford, Christow, Bridford, Teign Village STWs) and the **Dartmoor fringe**
  (Moretonhampstead, Lustleigh) account for the chronic high-duration works upstream.

## Caveats
- 2026 counts are partial-year (live feed) — evidence of *continuing* spills, not annual ranking.
- Rankings blend spill **frequency** and **duration**; both shown so neither hides the other.

## 4. Regulatory measures (WINEP)
The Teign **does** carry WINEP obligations (linked via water body / works), split improve vs monitor:

- **Improvement obligations — a fix is mandated, most due 31 Dec 2026 (this year):** a cluster of Teign
  Valley / Newton Abbot works carry UWWTR (`U_IMP1`) and Environment Act (`EnvAct_IMP2`) improvements —
  **Chudleigh STW, Christow STW, Chudleigh Knighton STW, Teign Village STW, Trusham STW, Bridford STW** —
  plus `U_IMP4` at **Compton Castle CSO** and **Main St, Abbotskerswell CSO**. Deadlines this year →
  press for/track on-time delivery.
- **Monitoring / investigation only:** Moretonhampstead, Dunsford, Heathfield, Lustleigh STWs and the
  Chagford CSOs (`U_MON3/4`, `EnvAct_INV4`) — measured, but **no committed reduction scheme**.
- **No WINEP measure at all:** **Summerhill SPS (7,050 hrs)**, Drewsteignton & Crockernwell STWs, and
  several Newton Abbot CSOs (Keyberry Rd, Buckland Rd, Forde Park) — heavy spillers with **no regulatory
  action recorded**. The clearest gap to raise with the EA/SWW.

## 5. Dry-weather spills (2026)
A dry-weather spill (no rain to excuse it) is presumptively non-compliant. **Note:** Teign event-level
data currently covers **2026 only** (no historical start/stop backfill), so these are this-year figures
and understate the longer record.

- **Chagford is the dry-weather hotspot:** **Meldon Rd CSO — all 5** of its 2026 spills were dry;
  **Chagford Fire 1 CSO — both** 2026 spills dry; Greenacres CSO — 1 dry. These Chagford CSOs carry
  **monitoring-only** WINEP — flagrant dry spilling, no fix ordered.
- **East Ogwell STW — all 3** of its 2026 spills dry: a 5,647-hour chronic works under Environment Act
  *investigation* (`EnvAct_INV4`) but **no improvement order**.
- **Chudleigh STW** — 2 dry in 2026, and it **does** hold a `U_IMP1`/`EnvAct_IMP2` improvement due
  **Dec 2026** — the one to watch for on-time delivery.

## The asks this supports
1. **Chagford CSOs (Meldon Rd, Chagford Fire, Greenacres)** — repeated *dry* spilling in 2026 with only
   monitoring: press for an improvement scheme, not just measurement.
2. **East Ogwell & Summerhill** — heavy chronic spillers with no improvement obligation (Summerhill has
   *no* WINEP at all): press to add them.
3. **The Dec-2026 improvement cluster (Chudleigh, Christow, Chudleigh Knighton, Teign Village, Trusham,
   Bridford)** — hold SWW to the deadline and verify spills actually fall.

## Driver glossary
`U_IMPn` = Urban Waste Water Regs improvement · `U_MON3/4` = UWWTR monitoring · `EnvAct_IMP2` =
Environment Act improvement · `EnvAct_INV4` = Environment Act investigation · `U_IMP4` = UWWTR improvement.
